## 互動圖表與資料集

### Strategy lens: theory evidence insight

#### vertical

##### zh

- theory: Williamson 的交易成本理論：資產專屬性高、市場不確定性大時，企業傾向垂直整合。Dunning 的 eclectic paradigm 亦強調，當知識與供應需內部保護時，應發揮 internalization advantage。
- evidence: 台塑 1301 約 62.01% 進貨來自台塑化 6505；四寶形成上（裂解）—中（1301 聚合）—下（南亞、台化應用）的集團內部鏈結。
- insight: 1301 的競爭力不是「會做 PVC」而已，而是被鎖定在集團內部市場中的成本、品質與開工率協調——這是制度性、難模仿的優勢。

##### en

- theory: Transaction cost economics (Williamson): high asset specificity and uncertainty favor vertical integration. Dunning's OLI highlights internalization when knowledge and supply must stay in-house.
- evidence: ~62% of 1301 purchases come from FPCC 6505; the four treasures form an intra-group upstream–midstream–downstream chain.
- insight: 1301's edge is not merely PVC capacity but being embedded in an internal market that coordinates cost, quality, and utilization—an institutional, hard-to-imitate advantage.

#### horizontal

##### zh

- theory: 集中多角化（Concentric/Related Diversification）與資源重配置：企業憑藉核心能力（化學合成與高分子技術）跨入新領域。這與水平多角化（以不相關技術進入原市場吸引舊客）不同。
- evidence: 台塑德山精密化學合資案（高雄雙氧水/異丙醇廠）；台塑新智能彰濱 LFP 電池芯與電解液項目；PAN 纖維升級至風電與儲氫瓶用高強度碳纖維。
- insight: 多角化並非偏離本業，而是動態能力（Dynamic Capabilities）的體現。透過與日本德山合資分攤技術風險，並將核心化工優勢延伸，台塑在高價值半導體與新能源材料上，利用 VRIO 資源築起高模仿壁壘。

##### en

- theory: Concentric/related diversification leverages core capabilities (chemical synthesis & polymer tech) for new markets, differing from horizontal diversification which targets existing customers with unrelated tech.
- evidence: FPC-Tokuyama JV for electronic chemicals; Formosa Smart Energy's Changbin LFP battery cell and electrolyte production; PAN fiber upgraded to specialized carbon fiber for wind power and hydrogen tanks.
- insight: Diversification exhibits dynamic capabilities. By sharing risks via JV and extending chemical resources, FPC establishes VRIO barriers in semiconductor and clean-tech material supply chains.

#### global

##### zh

- theory: OLI／折衷理論：Ownership（技術、規模）、Location（資源、市場、制度）、Internalization（是否內部化供應與知識）共同決定 FDI 與進入模式。
- evidence: 台灣：大金、旭化成、德山等技術尋求型合資；美國：1980 年代併購後德州／路易斯安那綠地；中國：寧波獨資園區＋集團內熱電與管線分工。
- insight: 國際擴張是「工具箱」思維：缺技術→合資；要資源與控制→美國綠地；要市場與規模→中國園區——而非單一模式走遍全球。

##### en

- theory: Dunning's OLI: ownership, location, and internalization advantages jointly shape FDI and entry mode choice.
- evidence: Taiwan: tech-seeking JVs; U.S.: 1980s M&A then shale-driven greenfield; China: Ningbo solo park plus intra-group utilities.
- insight: Internationalization is a toolkit: JV for technology, greenfield for resources/control, clustered parks for market scale—not one mode everywhere.

#### ma

##### zh

- theory: 市場進入模式：併購（M&A）以資本換取時間，快速獲得產能、客戶、執照；但須承擔整合、文化與負債風險（integration risk）。
- evidence: 1981 特拉華 PVC 廠、Stauffer 塑料部門；本土永嘉化學 PP 產能整合。其後美國以綠地擴大垂直整合。
- insight: 台塑在美國呈現「併購開門、綠地深化」的兩階段路徑——符合新興市場企業常見的漸進式國際化（incremental internationalization）。

##### en

- theory: M&A buys speed—capacity, customers, permits—but raises integration and cultural risk.
- evidence: 1981 Delaware PVC, Stauffer plastics assets, domestic Yung Chia PP deal; later U.S. greenfield deepened integration.
- insight: In the U.S., Formosa followed 'acquire to enter, build to integrate'—a phased, incremental internationalization path.

#### greenfield

##### zh

- theory: 進入模式權衡：綠地投資＝高控制、高資本、長回收期；策略聯盟／合資＝較低資本、共享風險，但控制與知識外流風險較高。
- evidence: 綠地：麥寮六輕、美國石化、寧波園區、彰濱 LFP；聯盟：成大 CCUS 產學、越南河靜鋼鐵與中鋼／JFE 合資。
- insight: 台塑在「核心石化＋高制度可預測」環境偏好綠地；在「跨業＋高政治／技術不確定」情境改用聯盟——這是理性的模式選擇，不是偏好問題。

##### en

- theory: Greenfield trades capital for control; alliances trade shared risk against control and knowledge spillover.
- evidence: Greenfield: Mailiao, U.S. petrochem, Ningbo, Changbin LFP; alliances: NCKU CCUS, Ha Tinh steel with CSC/JFE.
- insight: Greenfield fits core petrochemicals in predictable institutions; alliances fit cross-industry, high-uncertainty bets—rational mode matching.

#### future

##### zh

- theory: 制度壓力與利害關係人理論：碳費、CBAM、客戶 ESG 要求改變「合法性与資源」；動態能力要求企業重配資源（Teece）。
- evidence: 2024 首度全年虧損；外銷 72%；範疇一、二排放較 2020 年僅減 12.6%，低於 2025 年減 20% 目標；中國產能與 ECFA 變化。
- insight: 1301 需把碳成本內生化為投資與定價變數，並以產品／地區損益紀律淘汰長虧通用料——同時以集中多角化（半導體化學、儲能、碳纖）開第二曲線。

##### en

- theory: Institutional pressures (carbon fees, CBAM, ESG) reshape legitimacy and resources; dynamic capabilities require reconfiguration (Teece).
- evidence: First annual loss in 2024; exports 72%; emissions down only 12.6% vs 2020; China overcapacity and trade rule shifts.
- insight: 1301 must internalize carbon into pricing/capex, enforce P&L discipline on commodity lines, and pursue related diversification for a second growth curve.

### Vertical points

#### zh

1. 交易成本：內部化降低專屬資產被「敲竹槓」與斷供風險（相對仰賴中油的小塑化廠）。
2. 內部化優勢（OLI 之 I）：裂解後中間料在集團內流通，減少市場議價與品質不一致。
3. 規模經濟：六輕一貫化產能，使 1301 單位成本曲線優於外購原料競爭者。
4. 動態調度：油價／地緣衝擊時，集團可統籌庫存、開工率與產品組合，緩衝景氣。

#### en

1. Transaction costs: internalization cuts hold-up and supply disruption vs. CPC-dependent peers.
2. Internalization (OLI–I): post-cracking flows stay in-group, easing bargaining and quality risk.
3. Scale economies: Mailiao integration lowers unit costs vs. merchants buying feedstock.
4. Dynamic coordination: group can rebalance runs, inventory, and mix when oil or geopolitics shock.

### Diversification action nodes

#### 1. reason

- id: reason
- pos: tl
- zh: 轉型原因背景
- en: Drivers of Diversification
- shortZh: 內外部環境壓力
- shortEn: Market Drivers
- analysisZh: 【外部環境】：隨全球石化產業邁入成熟期，中國大陸同業大幅擴產 PVC、PE、PP 等泛用塑膠，引發結構性供給過剩與價格惡性競爭，導致傳統石化利潤邊際持續惡化；此外，ESG 減碳浪潮與新能源轉型，更迫使傳統石化企業加速尋求低碳高產值的第二成長曲線。

【內部策略】：台塑立足於深厚的高分子材料與化學合成能力，採取「集中多角化」路徑。此舉不僅能有效降低對單一傳統石化週期的依賴，更能將產品範疇延伸至半導體特用化學品、電子級材料與新能源電池核心供應鏈。

- analysisEn: Externally, the global petrochemical industry has entered maturity. Competitors in mainland China have aggressively expanded capacity in commodity plastics (PVC, PE, PP), causing structural oversupply and price wars that deteriorate traditional margins. Furthermore, ESG carbon reduction pressures and energy transition force companies to seek a second growth curve. Internally, FPC leverages its deep polymer materials and chemical synthesis capabilities to pursue concentric (related) diversification. This reduces cyclical dependence and extends product scope to semiconductor chemicals, electronic materials, and green energy battery supply chains.

#### 3. battery

FPC's shift from a carbon-heavy legacy to green energy storage exemplifies this dynamic capacity.

Strategy Concept: RBV asserts that to sustain competitive advantage, a firm must possess resources that are Valuable, Rare, Inimitable, and Non-substitutable (VRIO). FPC's carbon fiber capabilities constitute a capital- and technology-intensive rare asset, erecting high barriers against competitor imitation in lightweight energy materials.
