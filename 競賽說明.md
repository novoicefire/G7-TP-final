## C. Reminders of Webpage Project (網頁製作注意事項)

### a. Webpage Content Requirements (網頁內容)

The webpage content must include:

1.  **Team member introductions** (including the revised 2nd version of individual D-ID videos)
2.  **An Executive Summary**
3.  **Brief Introduction to the Company** from TP0
4.  **External & Industry Environment Analysis** from TP1
5.  **Internal Resources & Core Competencies** from TP2
6.  **Corporate Growth Strategy & Future Challenges** from TP3
7.  **A Conclusion**
8.  **Appendices** consisting of:
    - An interactive lab
    - Data sources
9.  **Webpage Footer:** Must include `"Instructor: Prof. Shihmin Lo"`.

We welcome any additional creative ideas or content beyond these requirements.  
_(網頁內容必須包含小組成員介紹 (含修改後的第二版 D-ID 影片)、綜合執行摘要、TP0-3 (包含公司簡介與現況、外部與產業環境分析、內部資源與核心能耐、集團成長策略與未來挑戰)、結論，以及互動實驗室、資料來源所組成之附錄。最後，網頁頁尾須加上指導教授：駱世民。我們歡迎任何超出上述要求之創意想法或內容。)_

### b. Webpage Features & Design (網頁功能與設計)

Please design a fully structured interactive webpage with the following features:

- **Mandarin/English bilingual switching** (中英雙語切換)
- **Light/dark mode settings** (明暗模式設定)
- **Audio guide** (語音導覽)
- **Interactivity** (互動性)
- **Glossary of terms** (專有名詞解釋)
- **Navigation Design:** Use a web menu and homepage navigation to improve usability. (網頁選單與首頁跳轉功能)
- **Responsive Web Design (RWD):** Ensure smooth browsing on any device. (響應式網頁設計)

### c. Audio Guide Configuration (語音導覽設定)

- Please check if your device has preset options.
  - **EN:** British-Male
  - **Mandarin:** Taiwan-Yating  
    _(語音導覽裝置須預設，EN＝British-Male；繁體中文＝Taiwan-Yating)_

### d. Media Usage (媒體使用)

- Images and videos can be used to enrich a webpage, but errors cannot be displayed if the webpage contains any images or videos.  
  _(可透過圖片或影片增加網頁豐富度，但若網頁中包含任何圖片或影片，絕對不能顯示錯誤或破圖)_

### e. Webpage Deployment (網頁部署)

- The webpage must be formally deployed online (e.g., via GitHub Pages or other hosting platforms) to ensure the overall completeness and professional presentation of the work. Please submit the live deployed link.  
  _(網頁必須正式部署上線 (例如透過 GitHub Pages 或其他代管平台)，以確保作品的完整性與專業呈現。請繳交部署後之正式網頁連結。)_

### f. Collaboration & Teamwork (團隊協作說明)

- Each member is jointly responsible for the overall quality and coherence of the webpage. Instead of dividing the work mechanically, your team should emphasize integration and collaboration.
- **Required Section:** Please include a brief section describing how members worked together and contributed to the project’s development, rather than listing individual tasks.  
  _(所有組員應共同負責網頁整體的品質與一致性，而非以分工方式切割任務。請於網頁中新增簡短說明區塊，來說明組員如何協作、整合並共同完成專案，而非僅列出各自負責項目。)_

### g. Visual Presentation & Language (視覺與語言呈現)

- Ensure clarity, smooth navigation, and balanced design.
- Your webpage must provide its overall content in both English and Mandarin, while any additional languages may be included as optional.
- Avoid lengthy text; instead, use visual and interactive elements effectively to convey ideas with clarity and impact.  
  _(網頁應保持清晰、順暢及美觀。整體內容須以中英文雙語呈現，其他語言為選配。內容不宜過長，應善用視覺與互動設計，以更有效率地傳達重點。)_

### h. Academic Integrity & AI Disclosures (學術誠信與 AI 揭露)

- All external materials and AI-generated content must comply with academic integrity and fair use for educational purposes.
- **AI Tools Disclosure:** Please clearly disclose which parts of your project used AI tools (e.g., writing assistance, transcript processing, or webpage design generation) and cite such tools appropriately. Also, indicate copyright acknowledgments where applicable.  
  _(所有外部資料與 AI 生成內容皆須符合學術誠信與教育合理使用原則。請主動揭露專案中使用 AI 工具之部分，例如撰文協助、逐字稿處理或網頁設計生成等，並適當註明所使用之工具與版權來源。)_
