Executive Summary

Formosa Plastics (1301) stands at a critical historical turning point. In 2024, facing massive Chinese capacity oversupply and cross-strait tariff suspensions (ECFA), FPC recorded its first annual loss. To secure future competitive advantages, the company is shifting from traditional low-cost commodity expansion to concentric diversification (semiconductors, LFP batteries, specialty carbon fiber) and strict green/carbon cost internalization.
